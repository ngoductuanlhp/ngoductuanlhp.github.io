Tuan Duc Ngo

AI Research Resident at VinAI Research.

Hi! I am an AI Research Resident at VinAI Research, advised by Dr. Khoi Nguyen. Prior to that, I received my B.E. degree in Computer Engineering from the Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, Vietnam, advised by Dr Dung Duc Nguyen and Dr. Hoang-Anh Pham.

My research interests are in the field of computer vision, with a specific focus on 3D understanding. I am developing algorithms and techniques for understanding the geometry and semantics of 3D scenes, with applications in autonomous driving, robotics, and augmented reality.
